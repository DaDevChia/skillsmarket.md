# Anything else

This was my first agent-driven hackathon.

I did not directly touch a line of code. My role was to architect the product, decide what mattered, give direction, judge the output, and keep redirecting the agents when something felt wrong.

That was the most interesting part of the project for me. Building became less about typing syntax and more about knowing what to ask for, checking whether the answer made sense, and deciding what was actually useful.
